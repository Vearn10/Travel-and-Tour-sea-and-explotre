About Us

Welcome to SEE And Explore Travel and Tours

See and Explore Travel and Tours was established in 2022 and officially registered in Calauan, Laguna, in 2023. Founded by Ms. Gerlie Eula, a young entrepreneur with a vision to provide quality travel assistance, focusing on solo and first-time travelers.

It has developed over time to offer safe and enjoyable journeys with personalized support, building trust for providing memorable experiences.

To be the most trusted travel preparation and booking agency for first-time and high-risk Filipino travelers, known for ethical guidance, personalized readiness coaching, and successful international departures.
